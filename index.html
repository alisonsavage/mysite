Invisible Ink

A print in which photos have been digitally manipulated to take the place of glass panes in a windowed hallway, as paintings would hang in a gallery. Further, each color photograph has been smoothed and altered in Photoshop, giving the images a somewhat other-worldly texture.



Dunnage Poster

A poster designed for Sam Ekwurtzel's November 2014 exhibit at the Jewett Gallery at Wellesley College, selected by the artist himself from a series of potential designs.



MFA Clearance Process Infographic

As an intern in the Intellectual Property Department of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston, I made an independent project of developing a clearer resource for future interns doing permissions research. This infographic provides a step-by-step path toward clearing works by any given artist for use by Museum.



iFound

Mobile-friendly web application, meant for use on college campuses to avoid students sending emails to thousands of individuals looking for lost items. iFound streamlines this process by allowing users to look at and write posts for lost objects as well as to post found items so that the owners may claim them. This project was developed with a hackathon team, on which I was in charge of design elements. As of now, the project is only completely functional in the front-end.
